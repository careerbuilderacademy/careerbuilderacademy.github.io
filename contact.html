CAREER BUILDER ACADEMY, Rajendra Nagar, Patna -16, established in 1992. The major objective of this institution is to impart education, through the medium of english and educate the youth in a spirit of service to the humanity. It priorities in education are not only scholastic excellence, but also formation of discipline in youth, hard work, morla values. these priorities are meant to prepare the youth for life, by promoting intellectual excellence, upright of character, emotional maturity, scientific temper, spirit of healthy competition, co-opertation and sportsmanship through co-curricular activities and national integration. Admission is open to all irrespective of religion, cast, creed or community. The school has a team of qualified and experienced teachers who are well versed in how to deal with children. Functional programmes such as general knowledge, Elocution drawing and painting contests and other related activities for cultural uplift of students are organised periodically. The school has a well established library with a collection of tandard and expensive books on different subjects.
